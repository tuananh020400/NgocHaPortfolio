Impact of Tight Monetary Policy on Credit Growth and Inflation in Vietnam (2020–2024)
📅 T3–T11/2024 👥 Group of 4 students 👨‍🏫 Dr. Nguyễn Văn Minh 🌟 Điểm B+ ⚙️ Stata 16 · VAR Model
Research Objectives
  • Analyze the relationship between refinancing rates and credit growth
  • Assess the impact of reserve requirements on bank liquidity
  • Examine the effectiveness of credit limits in controlling inflation
Methodology
  • Vector Autoregression (VAR) model
  • Quarterly time series data Q1/2020 – Q2/2024
  • Software: Stata 16, Excel
  • Variables: Interest rates, reserve requirements, credit, CPI
Key Findings
  • Refinancing rates negatively affect credit with a lag of 1–2 quarters
  • Reserve requirements strongly impact short-term liquidity
  • Credit limits are only moderately effective in controlling inflation
  • USD/VND exchange rate is positively correlated with inflation
Role & Lessons Learned
  • Responsible for collecting data from SBV, GSO, IMF
  • Ran VAR model and handled Granger causality test
  • Developed time series analysis skills in Stata
  • Teamwork and task allocation skills
